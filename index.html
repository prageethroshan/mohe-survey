The Ministry of Higher Education (MOHE) is developing a National Higher Education Policy to build a high-quality, inclusive, and future-ready education system in Sri Lanka. Your views and experiences as a student, graduate, employer, academic, or member of the public are essential to shape policies that ensure our graduates are skilled, employable, and globally competitive.

This anonymous survey takes about 10–15 minutes and will help identify key priorities and reforms for the higher education sector. Thank you for contributing to the future of higher education in Sri Lanka.
